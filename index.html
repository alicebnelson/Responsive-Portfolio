About Me

My first word was 'Cheese.' True Story. I went to James Madison University and majored in film and film production. While working at my first job out of university, I began to realize that the user experience of the customer could use a wee bit of improvement. I took classes at General Assembly for both Front End Web Development (to create more user friendly emails) and UX/UI (to make sure the user has the best possible experience). I found my love for front end design and UX/UI, however, I now want to round off my skills by going to UT's Coding Bootcamp. *May the coding gods grant me inspiration to tricky problems.*


Besides coding my current hobbies include watching terrible TV like Canada's Worst Driver and Canada's Worst Handyman (do you see a theme?). I am mother of two cats- Arya and Khaleesi. And yes, I love Game of Thrones too. I am ashamed to admit Buffalo Wild Wings is one of my favorite restaurants. Please don't judge too much.
